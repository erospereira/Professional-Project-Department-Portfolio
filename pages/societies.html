Computer Societies

What Are Computer Societies?

Computer societies are student organizations that provide opportunities to network, collaborate, and learn within the field of computer science. These societies aim to foster innovation, leadership, and skill development among their members.

Available Societies at BYU-Idaho

  • Association for Computing Machinery (ACM): The ACM chapter focuses on advancing computing as a science and profession. Members participate in coding challenges, research projects, and workshops. Learn more about ACM.
  • Society for Women Engineers (SWE): SWE aims to empower women in technology by providing mentorship and leadership opportunities. Join to be part of hackathons, seminars, and advocacy initiatives. Visit SWE.
  • Artificial Intelligence Society: Dedicated to exploring machine learning, AI ethics, and hands-on projects, this society is perfect for those passionate about AI. Members engage in competitions and collaborative research.
  • Amateur Radio Club: Bridging technology and communication, this society provides opportunities to explore wireless technology and its applications.
  • Cybersecurity Club: Focuses on ethical hacking, security awareness, and preparing for CTF (Capture the Flag) competitions. Explore Cybersecurity at BYU-Idaho.

Benefits of Joining

Joining a computer society can help you:

  • Build professional networks with peers and industry professionals.
  • Enhance your technical and soft skills through workshops and events.
  • Access exclusive resources such as mentorship programs and internships.
  • Develop leadership skills by organizing events and leading teams.
  • Participate in competitions and showcase your skills to potential employers.

How to Get Involved

  1. Check out our Upcoming Events page for society meetings and activities.
  2. Contact society leaders through the university directory or their official emails.
  3. Visit the club booth during student fairs or open house events.
  4. Follow the societies on social media for updates and announcements.

Success Stories

Many students who joined computer societies have successfully secured internships and jobs at leading tech companies. Alumni often return to share their experiences and mentor current members, creating a supportive community.
