About the Department of Professional Projects

Our Mission

The Department of Professional Projects at BYU-Idaho is dedicated to bridging the gap between academia and industry by offering students hands-on learning opportunities. Through internships, senior projects, and collaborative events, our mission is to prepare students for successful careers by enhancing their practical and technical skills.

Programs and Opportunities

  • Senior Projects: A capstone experience where students tackle real-world challenges provided by industry partners. Students work in teams to develop innovative solutions, gaining valuable technical and collaboration skills.
  • Internship Program: Facilitates connections with top companies, providing students with practical experience and a chance to explore career paths. Learn more about internships.
  • Hackathons and Competitions: Events like hackathons encourage creativity and innovation. Students collaborate in fast-paced environments to create unique solutions to technical problems. See upcoming events.
  • Faculty Portfolios: A showcase of faculty achievements, research, and professional contributions that inspire and guide students. View faculty portfolios.
  • Professional Societies: Join student-led societies such as the ACM, SWE, or Cybersecurity Club to network and build skills. Explore societies.

Why Choose Us?

Our department is committed to student success by providing:

  • Strong industry connections for internships and project sponsorships.
  • Experienced faculty mentors who guide students through every step of their journey.
  • Resources to help students build competitive portfolios, resumes, and career strategies.
  • Opportunities to engage in collaborative and interdisciplinary projects.
