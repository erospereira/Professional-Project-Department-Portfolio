Internship Opportunities

Why Pursue an Internship?

Internships provide invaluable real-world experience, allowing you to apply classroom knowledge in professional settings. They enhance your resume, build your professional network, and often lead to full-time employment opportunities.

Available Internship Programs

  • Microsoft University Internship: A 12-week program offering roles in Cloud Solutions Architecture, Customer Success Management, and more. Learn more.
  • Amazon Operations Internship: A 10-week program combining leadership development and experiential learning. Learn more.
  • Google STEP Internship: A 12-week program for first and second-year undergraduate students with a passion for computer science. Learn more.

How to Apply

  1. Update your resume to highlight relevant skills and experiences.
  2. Prepare a compelling cover letter tailored to the internship.
  3. Gather letters of recommendation from professors or previous employers.
  4. Submit your application through the company's career portal before the deadline.

University Resources

BYU-Idaho offers resources to assist you in securing internships:

  • Career Services: Provides resume reviews, mock interviews, and job search assistance. Visit Career Services.
  • Handshake: The university's platform for job and internship listings. Access Handshake.
