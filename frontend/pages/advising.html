Academic Advising

Meet Your Academic Advisor

Our academic advisors are here to assist you in planning your educational journey, selecting courses, and preparing for your career. Schedule an appointment to discuss your goals and ensure you're on track for graduation.

How to Schedule an Appointment

  • Call us at (208) 496-1411
  • Email us at advising@byui.edu
  • Schedule online through I-Plan
  • Visit our office in the Manwaring Center (across from the University Store)

Preparing for Your Appointment

  • Think about your educational and career goals
  • Write down any questions in advance
  • Arrive on time and take notes
  • Ask questions when you don't understand
  • Schedule a follow-up appointment if necessary
