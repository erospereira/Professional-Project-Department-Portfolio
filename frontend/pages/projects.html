Senior Projects

Overview

Senior projects are a culmination of your academic journey, allowing you to apply your knowledge to real-world challenges. These projects demonstrate your skills to potential employers and contribute to your professional portfolio.

Project Submission Guidelines

  1. Project Proposal: Submit a detailed proposal outlining your project's objectives, methodology, and expected outcomes.
  2. Faculty Advisor Approval: Obtain approval from a faculty advisor who will guide you through the project.
  3. Regular Updates: Provide bi-weekly progress reports to your advisor.
  4. Final Presentation: Present your project findings at the end of the semester.
  5. Documentation: Submit a comprehensive report and any relevant code or materials.

Past Project Examples

  • Automated Irrigation System: Developed a system to optimize water usage for agricultural applications.
  • Mobile Health App: Created an application to monitor and manage chronic health conditions.
